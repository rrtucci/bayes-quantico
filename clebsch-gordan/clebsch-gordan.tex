Let $b^{nb}=(b_1, b_2, \ldots, b_{nb})$ where $b_i\in Z_{[0,db_i]}$  and $a\in Z_{[1,d_\lam]}$.
Hence,

\beq
d_\lam = \prod_{i=1}^{nb}db_i
\eeq

\beq
(C_\lam)_{a}^ {b^{nb}}=
\bcen
\xymatrix{
&&b_1\ar[dl]
\\
a
& C_\lam\ar[l]
&b_2\ar[l]
\\
&&b_{nb}\ar[lu]
}
\ecen
\eeq

\beq

\bcen

\eeq

may lowered and their arrows
changed to outgoing instead of ingoing.

represents a different

\eeq
